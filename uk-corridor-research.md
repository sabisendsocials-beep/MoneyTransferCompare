# UK Money Transfer Provider Research

## Research for UK to Kenya (GBP → KES)

### Major Providers Supporting UK-Kenya:
1. **Wise (formerly TransferWise)** - API available, competitive rates
2. **WorldRemit** - Strong presence in Kenya, mobile money integration
3. **Remitly** - Growing Kenya operations, cash pickup available
4. **Western Union** - Extensive agent network in Kenya
5. **MoneyGram** - Traditional operator with Kenya presence
6. **Azimo** - European-focused, supports Kenya
7. **Sendwave** - Africa-focused, popular for Kenya transfers
8. **Small World** - UK-based, Kenya corridor available

### Kenya-Specific Features:
- M-Pesa mobile money integration (WorldRemit, Remitly)
- Bank deposits to major Kenyan banks
- Cash pickup locations in major cities

## Research for UK to India (GBP → INR)

### Major Providers Supporting UK-India:

3. **BookMyForex** - India-focused platform
4. **Remitly** - Growing India presence
5. **Western Union** - Traditional operator
6. **MoneyGram** - Established India network
7. **Xoom (PayPal)** - Strong India operations
8. **IndusInd Bank** - Direct bank transfer specialist
9. **ICICI Bank Money2India** - Bank-specific service

### India-Specific Features:
- Direct bank deposits to all major Indian banks
- Cash pickup at bank branches
